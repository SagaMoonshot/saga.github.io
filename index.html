About Saga.

Saga envisions a future where every single learner that goes through the education system is able to make their chidhood dreams a reality.

We are a company that exists as a platform for all students, creating a personalized curriculum that is unique to their personal goals. Saga has created a platform that has the ability to find and create content, assignments, and lessons that match each indviuals need and preferences. The students will be immersed in our system throughout their active education, and once they have completed their Saga, they will have the skills and knowledge to perform in their feild of interest.

Objectives.

In order to best help students, as a company we have six objectives that are listed below.

Integration With School Life

Partnering with school boards, discussing benifits with teachers, using Machine Learning + Web Scrapping to gather content. Teachers will then be able to upload it and also use it to aid with homework.

Make Learning Interesting

Create personalized reasources that meet the specific indiviuals interests. Teach the indiviual in a way that they can see the larger impact on the world about what they are learning.

Changing The Idea Of School

We want to get rid of the idea in many students mind that school is 'boring' or 'not useful'. We want to create a realization withen the students that the simple things they learn can change the world positvely.

Making The Saga Platform Accessible

In order to allow all students to use this platform, it is going to be free.

Helping Students Learn Joyfully

Creating a personalized cirriculum so that the student does not feel like they are being forced to learn.

Helping Students Prepare For The Future

Teaching students in a way that also develops skills for their future.

Key Features.

Below we have outlined Saga's key features.

Personalized Cirriculum

By collecting data on the student's skills, interests, and stregths with their permission, Saga will have the ability to create a full student-strutured curriculum. For students that have yet to find their passion, Saga is here to help with that.

Content Creation

Saga will have the ability to create lessons, assignments, and practice problems specific for students by implementing General Adverserial Netowrks (GANs) and Natrual Language Processing (NLP). Lessons can be in a video format or written format, depending on which method the student learns best from.

Evaluation + Feedback

Feedback is a key factor to improving skills, and through using a semantic parsing and feature extraction, Saga can provide insights on writte connect. Saga also will have the ability to evaluate math world problems.
